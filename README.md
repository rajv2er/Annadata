# Annadata

##  Project Overview
**Annadata** is a decentralized agricultural supply chain traceability platform. 

The goal is to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers by providing an immutable record of produce origin, ensuring fair prices for farmers and authenticity for buyers.

##  Key Features
*   **Multi-Stakeholder Dashboard:** Dedicated interfaces for Farmers, Distributors, Retailers, Consumers, and Government officials.
*   **Chain of Custody:** Step-by-step digital tracking from harvest to sale.
*   **QR Connectivity:** End-consumers can scan product QR codes to view the entire journey of their food.
*   **Government Oversight:** Real-time analytics on production volumes and regional distribution.
